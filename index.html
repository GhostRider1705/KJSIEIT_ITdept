ABOUT

US

Department of Information Technology was established in the year 2001-02 for imparting the quality based technical education in the IT field with constant value addition in the frontiers of Computer and Information Science. The strength of the department is the students and the faculty. Department has Faculty members constantly upgrade themselves so as to keep track of the fast changes in the field either by pursuing higher education or attending various conferences, workshops or training programs. Department has 12 faculty members, 4 Lab Assistants and 2 Lab Attendants. Our students are also provided platform to improve their skills by organizing various activities under the banner of IEEE, IETE, and CSI, annual technical and cultural programs.
